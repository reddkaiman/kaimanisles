About the Game
Brew fast. Serve faster.

Instant Estresso puts you behind the counter of your very own espresso bar. Take orders from a stream of caffeine-desperate customers, craft their drinks with precision, and try to keep up with demand as the difficulty ramps up.

The game blends the fast-paced order-juggling of classic diner management games with the tactile satisfaction of actually making drinks — pulling shots, choosing milks, layering flavors, and sliding finished orders across the counter before the timer runs out.

Unlike typical coffee shop simulators that lean toward slow-paced management, Instant Estresso is immediate and skill-based. Every drink is a micro-puzzle. Every shift is a sprint. One wrong ingredient and your streak resets.

The entire game — code, art, character design, and a full original lo-fi café soundtrack — was built from scratch by a single developer.

Key Features
What makes it different.
☕

Drag & Drop Brewing

Craft drinks with intuitive touch controls. Pull espresso shots, steam milk, and layer flavors with satisfying precision. Every action is a deliberate choice, not a menu tap.

👦

Multiple Baristas

Unlock and play as different characters, each with their own personality and style behind the counter. Find the barista that matches your play style.

📈

Progressive Difficulty

Start with simple americanos. Work your way up to complex multi-ingredient specialty drinks under intense time pressure. The difficulty curve keeps you in the zone.

🎵

Original Soundtrack

Every track composed and produced by the developer. Lo-fi café beats that make the chaos feel cozy. Not stock music — a real album made for this game.

🏆

Three Difficulty Modes

Easy for casual vibes. Medium for a challenge. Hard for those who want to feel what a real morning rush is like. Each mode plays like a different game.

📷

Built Solo

Code, art, music, sound design, and business — all by one person. An indie game in the truest sense. Every pixel has a fingerprint on it.
